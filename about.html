Asked Questions

What does 'Asgi' mean?

Its a reference to a character in the Kesh Temple Hymn, one of the 2 oldest surving works of literature in the world. I've had a fair amount of interest in Sumerian history and literature over the years. I am also a big fan of alliteration and symmetries in names. So when I was trying to come up with a pseudonym, I thought it seemed decent. I also find it funny that the character is praised as such an amazing hero in each verse, but the only thing he actually does in the text is drink some wine. For reference, the other oldest work is the Instructions of Shuruppak.
